What's Magen?

Magen, or Magnetic field generator, is a programming project that tries to simulates magnets, their power and movements.

Actually it's (in public version) possible to simulate magnets in a 2D space. Every magnet is turned with its poles perpendiculary to the plane, so the magnetic field is radiated in cycles.

Impelented features: magnets(power, weight, polarity), GUI(strips density, strips color), render(window, file)

Render speed: HD quality is rendered at speed 3 frames per second. Once it will be completly rewrited to run on a graphic card.
